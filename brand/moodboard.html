02 Logo System

A wordmark that blends, a mark that meters.

The x is a crossfade — two waveform strokes crossing as one fades in and the other out. The standalone mark is three rising EQ bars (40 / 70 / 100%): a kin to Doremi's three waves, redrawn as an energy meter.

03 Color

Ink and mist. The spectrum only where energy lives.

One warm-to-cool energy spectrum carries all the chroma. Everything else is near-black and warm grey, so the curve can sing. One gradient per view, max.

07 Anti-slop Guardrails

What this brand refuses to be.

Altitude: Linear, Teenage Engineering, a good Boiler Room site. Restrained, technical, music-first, a little cold. Show the product, never a mascot.

✕
No glowing neon headphonesThe cliché DJ hero. We show the curve and the decks.
✕
No AI-purple templateGeneric SaaS gradient-on-black. Our chroma is warm-to-cool, rationed.
✕
No stock DJ clip-artNo turntable photography, no festival crowd shots.
✕
No rounded-blob illustrationsNo floating gradient bubbles. Geometry, lines, meters.
✕
No emoji headings, no ✨The machine voice is mono, not decorated.
✕
No mascot or persona nameThe AI is just "Doremix" — or "the conductor" in docs.
✕
No two glows per viewOne gradient, one play state. If two things shine, one is wrong.
✕
No "revolutionary" copyNo buzzwords, no em-dash slop, no enterprise SaaS tone.
