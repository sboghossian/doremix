03 Color

05 Visual Language

Glass, glow, and moving light.

Frosted glass over a drifting gradient-mesh, colored glow on the live element, big gradient-filled display type, rounded geometry. Lovable-flavored and techy, but crafted. Motion is springy and means something.

Glassmorphism
6% white fill, 16px blur, 12% hairline, 16–24px radius, inset top highlight. Friendly, not boxy.

08 Vivid, not slop

Colorful and techy — but designed.

Turn the color up without sliding into template territory. Altitude: Lovable.dev, Framer showcase, a vivid Vercel gradient moment, Boiler Room after dark.

✓
Full six-stop spectrum
Use all six club lights. Never a flat single-purple gradient — that's the AI-slop tell.
✓
Motion must mean something
Beat-reactive glow, energy-arc draw, mesh drift. No decorative wobble for its own sake.
✓
Show the product
The lit curve, the decks, the cue sheet, the sets dashboard. Never a mascot or persona.
✕
No stock headphone photos
No glowing neon headphones, turntable clip-art, or festival crowd shots. We draw our own light.
✕
Glow restraint
One or two glows per view. If everything shines, nothing does. Live green = playing only.
✕
No buzzword / emoji copy
No "revolutionary", no ✨ in product copy, no em-dash slop. Founder-direct, after-dark.
